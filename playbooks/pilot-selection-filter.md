# Pilot Selection Filter

Choose pilots that are easy to measure, meaningful to the business, and safe enough to learn from quickly.

## Strong pilot characteristics

- high workflow volume
- visible pain today
- clear baseline available
- existing system data
- named business owner
- reversible outcomes
- human review already exists or can be added

## Weak pilot characteristics

- low-frequency executive work
- unclear definition of success
- no obvious source system
- highly political workflow with no clean baseline
- difficult-to-detect quality failure
- no owner accountable for results

## Quick scoring

Score each candidate workflow from 1 to 5 on:

- business value
- measurement readiness
- governance safety
- change readiness
- scale potential

Prefer workflows with high business value and measurement readiness before choosing the most glamorous use case.
