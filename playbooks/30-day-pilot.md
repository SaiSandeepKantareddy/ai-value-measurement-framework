# 30-Day Pilot Playbook

## Goal

Prove measurable value in one workflow with a lightweight but credible operating model.

## Week 1

- choose one workflow with visible business pain
- define the workflow start and completion events
- document the current baseline
- assign an executive sponsor and workflow owner

## Week 2

- instrument core events
- define 4 to 6 metrics across value and risk
- establish review expectations
- prepare the first weekly scorecard

## Week 3

- launch the pilot with a controlled group
- review adoption, overrides, and quality signals
- fix workflow or UX issues before scaling

## Week 4

- compare against baseline
- summarize business meaning, not just metric movement
- decide whether to scale, redesign, or stop

## Exit criteria

- measurable improvement in at least one core business metric
- no unacceptable governance breaches
- clear understanding of why the pilot did or did not work
