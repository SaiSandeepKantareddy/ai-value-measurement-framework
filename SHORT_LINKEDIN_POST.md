# Short LinkedIn Post

Most companies are measuring AI wrong.

They track activity:

- prompts
- chat volume
- lines of code

But activity is not value.

I put together an open-source **AI Value Measurement Framework** to help teams measure AI across:

- adoption
- speed
- quality
- throughput
- leverage
- risk and governance

It is built for engineering, support, analytics, ops, and internal copilots.

Repo:
`https://github.com/SaiSandeepKantareddy/ai-value-measurement-framework`

The core idea:

AI does not fail because models are weak.
It fails because organizations do not know how to measure value.
