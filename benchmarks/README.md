# Benchmark Bands

Use directional interpretation bands when exact industry benchmarks are unavailable.

## Adoption

- weak: below 20 percent of eligible users in target workflows
- emerging: 20 to 40 percent
- healthy: 40 to 70 percent
- leading: above 70 percent

## Override rate

- weak: very high override with no explanation or no segmentation
- emerging: override tracked but not yet tied to workflow or model conditions
- healthy: override segmented and used for product improvement
- leading: override trends inform scale and governance decisions

## Scorecard maturity

- weak: metrics without baseline
- emerging: workflow-level baseline but no executive review
- healthy: monthly review with decisions
- leading: portfolio-level governance and scale logic

These bands are not universal truth. They are intended to help leaders interpret direction and maturity.
