# AI Measurement Governance

Measurement should help organizations scale safely, not just prove adoption.

## Governance requirements by workflow criticality

### Low criticality

- periodic quality spot checks
- lightweight acceptance tracking

### Medium criticality

- defined review owner
- override tracking
- exception logging

### High criticality

- mandatory human review
- clear approval chain
- policy and audit evidence retention
- escalation path for unsafe outputs

## Control questions

- Is the workflow experimental or approved for production use?
- What type of human review is required?
- What data sensitivity level applies?
- What event logs must be retained?
- What threshold should trigger a pause?
