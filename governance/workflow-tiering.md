# Workflow Tiering

Use workflow tiers to align measurement rigor and control strength.

## Tier 1

Low-risk internal productivity workflows with reversible outcomes.

## Tier 2

Operational workflows with moderate customer or business impact.

## Tier 3

High-risk workflows involving compliance, financial impact, sensitive data, or externally visible decisions.

## Guidance

- higher tiers require stronger review and auditability
- do not compare metrics across tiers without context
