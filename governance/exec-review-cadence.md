# Executive Review Cadence

## Weekly operating review

Focus on:

- adoption movement
- instrumentation gaps
- override spikes
- workflow-specific issues

## Monthly business review

Focus on:

- baseline versus current performance
- business interpretation
- quality and risk tradeoffs
- candidate workflows for expansion

## Quarterly portfolio review

Focus on:

- funding decisions
- scale versus pause decisions
- policy and control health
- cross-functional learning
