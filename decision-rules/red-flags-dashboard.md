# Red Flags Dashboard

Do not scale a workflow just because it looks active.

## Immediate red flags

- adoption rising while quality falls
- policy exceptions increasing
- high override rate with no diagnosis
- weak review coverage on medium- or high-risk workflows
- no agreed business interpretation for scorecard changes
- time-saved claims without observed evidence

## Yellow flags

- strong pilot results in one team only
- large speed gains with no throughput or quality movement
- unclear workflow ownership
- measurement arguments consuming more time than the workflow itself

## Executive response

When red flags appear:

- pause expansion
- verify instrumentation
- inspect real workflow behavior
- require a mitigation plan before scaling resumes
