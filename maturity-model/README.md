# AI Value Measurement Maturity Model

This model helps organizations understand where they are today and what the next step should be.

## Level 1: Activity Tracking

Typical behavior:

- tracks prompts, chats, or licenses
- reports anecdotal time saved
- has no workflow baseline

Risks:

- rewards activity over value
- cannot support executive decisions

## Level 2: Workflow Measurement

Typical behavior:

- chooses priority workflows
- tracks adoption, speed, and quality in those workflows
- starts to compare assisted and unassisted work

Risks:

- metrics may still be fragmented across teams
- governance may lag behind usage

## Level 3: Business Outcome Linkage

Typical behavior:

- links workflow metrics to team or business outcomes
- uses regular scorecards and review cadences
- includes control metrics like override and policy exceptions

Risks:

- inconsistent standards between functions
- scaling decisions still depend on manual interpretation

## Level 4: Portfolio Governance and ROI

Typical behavior:

- runs one operating model across multiple functions
- reviews AI value, risk, and rollout decisions at portfolio level
- uses common definitions, integrations, and executive scorecards

Signals of strength:

- common KPI language across teams
- standard event schema
- executive review loop with decision rules
- clear scale, pause, and retire decisions

## How to use the model

1. Identify your current level honestly.
2. Standardize the minimum practices required for the next level.
3. Do not skip governance while scaling.
4. Upgrade only after at least one workflow shows real measured value.
