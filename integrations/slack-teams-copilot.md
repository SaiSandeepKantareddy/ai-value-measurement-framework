# Slack, Teams, and Internal Copilot Integration

## Best for

Knowledge workflows where employees use AI to draft content, summarize context, answer questions, or locate internal information.

## Watch-out

Raw chat volume is not a value metric. The useful measurement unit is the workflow outcome enabled by the interaction.

## Stronger metrics

- percent of target workflows assisted
- time to first draft
- completion rate for assisted tasks
- human override rate
- confidence or review requirement by content type
