# Zendesk + Salesforce Integration

## Best for

Support workflows that combine customer case handling, reply drafting, knowledge retrieval, and escalation management.

## Source-of-truth mapping

- case intake: Zendesk or Salesforce ticket creation
- workflow progress: status transitions, assignment, escalation
- quality: QA score, first-contact resolution, CSAT
- governance: policy exceptions, compliance reviews

## Useful events

- case created
- AI draft generated
- AI draft accepted or edited
- case resolved
- customer follow-up received
- QA review completed
- exception flagged
