# LinkedIn Launch Post

Most companies are measuring AI wrong.

They track activity:

- prompts
- chat volume
- lines of code
- story points

But leaders do not actually care about activity.
They care about value.

That is why I put together an open-source **AI Value Measurement Framework**:

`https://github.com/SaiSandeepKantareddy/ai-value-measurement-framework`

It is designed to help teams measure AI across:

- adoption
- speed
- quality
- throughput
- leverage
- risk and governance

The point is simple:

AI does not fail because models are weak.
It fails because organizations do not know how to measure value.

This repo includes:

- KPI taxonomy
- scorecard templates
- executive review templates
- rollout playbooks
- instrumentation guidance
- decision rules for scale, fix, pause, or retire

I wanted to make this practical enough that a leader, manager, or AI platform team could use it right away across engineering, support, analytics, ops, and internal copilots.

If you are building or scaling AI internally, I would love feedback.
