# Carousel Outline

## Slide 1

Title:
Most Companies Are Measuring AI Wrong

Subtitle:
They track activity, not value.

## Slide 2

Title:
What Most Teams Measure

Body:

- prompts sent
- chat volume
- lines of code
- story points

Footer:
Easy to count. Weak as business proof.

## Slide 3

Title:
What Leaders Actually Need To Know

Body:

- Is AI improving important workflows?
- Is quality holding?
- Is risk under control?
- Should we scale or pause?

## Slide 4

Title:
The 6 Pillars Of AI Value Measurement

Body:

- adoption
- speed
- quality
- throughput
- leverage
- risk and governance

## Slide 5

Title:
Start With A Workflow, Not A Model

Body:

- pick one workflow
- define the baseline
- instrument events
- review outcomes weekly

## Slide 6

Title:
Good AI Measurement Looks Like This

Body:

- baseline before rollout
- outcome metrics over activity
- quality plus speed
- decision rules for scale or pause

## Slide 7

Title:
Why AI Programs Fail

Body:

- high adoption, low value
- weak instrumentation
- hidden downstream rework
- no governance layer

## Slide 8

Title:
I Open-Sourced A Framework For This

Body:

- scorecards
- templates
- rollout playbooks
- instrumentation guides
- executive reviews

Footer:
GitHub: `ai-value-measurement-framework`

## Slide 9

Title:
The Core Idea

Body:

AI does not fail because models are weak.
It fails because organizations do not know how to measure value.
