# Operations Example Scorecard

## Workflow

Back-office case handling and exception processing with AI assistance for routing, summarization, and action drafting.

## Example metrics

| Pillar | Metric | Baseline | Target | Example interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adoption | % of operators using AI in target queues weekly | 22% | 60% | Adoption is building in the highest-volume workflows |
| Speed | Median case processing time | 18 min | 14 min | AI is reducing navigation and drafting time |
| Quality | Accuracy after human review | 93% | 96% | Speed gains are holding quality |
| Throughput | Cases closed per operator per day | 29 | 34 | More work is being completed without added headcount |
| Leverage | % of exceptions resolved without escalation | 48% | 57% | Frontline teams can absorb more complex work |
| Risk/Governance | Control breach count | 4 per month | 0 to 1 | Automation must stay within policy limits |
