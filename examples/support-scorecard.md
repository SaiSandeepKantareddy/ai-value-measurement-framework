# Support Example Scorecard

## Workflow

Tier 1 support agents using AI for case summarization, suggested replies, and knowledge retrieval.

## Example metrics

| Pillar | Metric | Baseline | Target | Example interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adoption | % of agents using AI in target queues weekly | 35% | 70% | Consistent usage is required before impact can compound |
| Speed | Average handle time | 13.8 min | 11.5 min | AI is reducing drafting and lookup time |
| Quality | First-contact resolution | 66% | 72% | Faster responses are still solving the issue |
| Throughput | Cases resolved per agent per day | 18 | 21 | Team capacity rises without adding headcount |
| Leverage | % of cases resolved without escalation | 54% | 61% | AI is helping frontline agents absorb more complexity |
| Risk/Governance | Policy exception rate | 2.4% | <1.5% | Governance must improve as automation rises |

## Notes

- Pair handle time with customer outcome metrics.
- Track when agents ignore or rewrite AI drafts.
- Review policy exceptions manually until the workflow is stable.
