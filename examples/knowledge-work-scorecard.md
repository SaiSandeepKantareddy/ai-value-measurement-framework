# Knowledge Work Example Scorecard

## Workflow

Internal content drafting, summarization, and knowledge retrieval using an enterprise copilot.

## Example metrics

| Pillar | Metric | Baseline | Target | Example interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adoption | % of approved users active weekly in target workflows | 30% | 65% | The copilot is being used for real work, not just experimentation |
| Speed | Time to first draft | 90 min | 45 min | Drafting is faster on repeatable content |
| Quality | Human acceptance rate | 52% | 70% | Output quality is improving enough to reduce rewrite load |
| Throughput | Documents completed per month | 120 | 150 | More useful output is getting finished |
| Leverage | % of manager time reallocated from drafting to review | 18% | 30% | Senior time shifts toward higher-value judgment |
| Risk/Governance | Sensitive-content exception rate | 1.8% | <1% | Scale depends on keeping usage within policy bounds |
