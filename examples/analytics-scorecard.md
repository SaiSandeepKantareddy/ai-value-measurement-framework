# Analytics Example Scorecard

## Workflow

Business analysts using AI to accelerate SQL drafting, narrative synthesis, and dashboard preparation.

## Example metrics

| Pillar | Metric | Baseline | Target | Example interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adoption | % of analysts using AI weekly in approved workflows | 28% | 65% | Usage is expanding into real analysis work |
| Speed | Median turnaround for ad hoc requests | 4.5 days | 3 days | Decision support is arriving faster |
| Quality | Revision rounds per deliverable | 2.3 | 1.6 | First drafts are closer to stakeholder expectations |
| Throughput | Deliverables completed per analyst per month | 11 | 14 | Team capacity improves without sacrificing rigor |
| Leverage | % of analyst time spent on interpretation vs preparation | 32% | 45% | AI shifts effort toward higher-value thinking |
| Risk/Governance | Data quality review failure rate | 5% | <3% | Faster output must still meet trust standards |

## Notes

- Measure business decision adoption where possible.
- Keep human review visible in the scorecard.
- Avoid counting query volume as a proxy for value.
